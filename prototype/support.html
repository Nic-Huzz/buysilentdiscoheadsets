Support

Shipping, returns, warranty, and customer support

Shipping Information

Where We Ship From

All orders ship from our logistics hub in Shenzhen, China, ensuring fast dispatch and reliable global delivery.

Carriers We Use

We partner with reputable, fully-tracked couriers including DHL Express, FedEx, UPS, and leading regional delivery partners.

Tracking information is provided as soon as your order ships.

Customs & Import Duties

We cover all customs duties, import taxes, and clearance fees whenever possible.

In rare cases where duties cannot be prepaid in your region:

  • We will notify you before your order ships, and
  • We will deduct the duty cost from your purchase price, or
  • Guide you through any local customs steps required.

Our goal is to make international shipping as smooth and cost-free as possible for you.

Returns

We do not offer change-of-mind returns.

Because our headsets are used for events, retreats, and commercial operations, and require hygiene and electronic integrity, we can only accept returns for defective products.

If your product arrives faulty, damaged, or not functioning correctly, we will repair or replace it under our 1-year warranty (see below).

1-Year Warranty

All headsets and transmitters come with a 12-month limited warranty covering:

  • Manufacturer defects
  • Electrical or audio malfunctions
  • Performance issues not caused by misuse

How to Make a Warranty Claim

  1. Email us at huzz@nichuzz.com
  2. Include your order number
  3. Describe the issue and attach photos or a short video
  4. Our team will respond within 1–3 business days with next steps

What's Not Covered

  • Damage from drops, impact, or misuse
  • Liquid damage
  • Improper charging or use of non-approved power sources
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Normal wear and tear (scratches, scuffs)
  • Loss, theft, or cosmetic damage that does not affect performance
