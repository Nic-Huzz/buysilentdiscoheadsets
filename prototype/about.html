About Us

From Bali's largest silent disco rental business to empowering facilitators worldwide

Our Story

Buy Silent Disco Headsets was founded by Nic "Huzz" in Bali after running hundreds of silent disco, breathwork, and guided experiences across Australia and Southeast Asia.

Our mission is simple:
Make high-quality silent disco technology accessible, reliable, and effortless for facilitators and event creators — so you can focus on delivering transformational experiences.

From intimate retreats to large-scale dance activations, our headsets have powered thousands of moments of joy, connection, and emotion.

Meet the Team
